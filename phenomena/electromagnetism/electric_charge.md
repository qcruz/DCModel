## Observation

Electric charge is quantized in units of e = 1.602 × 10⁻¹⁹ C. All observed charges
are integer multiples of e/3 (quark charges) or e (lepton charges). Charge is exactly
conserved. The proton and electron charges are equal and opposite to one part in 10²¹.

---

## Standard Explanation

U(1)_EM gauge charge. Quantization arises from the fact that representations of U(1)
are labeled by integers. Charge conservation follows from U(1) gauge symmetry (Noether's
theorem).
