## Observation

Electric charges attract or repel with force F = kq₁q₂/r². Moving charges create magnetic
fields. Changing magnetic fields create electric fields (induction). Together, electric and
magnetic fields propagate as electromagnetic waves at speed c. Maxwell's four equations
unify all of this. The force is infinite-range and falls off as 1/r².

massless photon (Yukawa potential → Coulomb potential as mass → 0).
