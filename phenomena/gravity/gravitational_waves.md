## Observation

Gravitational waves are ripples in spacetime produced by accelerating masses. First
detected by LIGO in 2015 from a binary black hole merger. They travel at c (confirmed
to 10⁻¹⁵). They stretch and compress space transversely. The strain h ~ 10⁻²¹ at Earth
from GW150914. Binary pulsars lose energy at the rate predicted by GR (Nobel 1993).
