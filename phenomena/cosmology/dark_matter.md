## Observation

~27% of the universe's energy content is dark matter — matter that interacts gravitationally
but not electromagnetically (does not emit, absorb, or reflect light). Evidence: galaxy
rotation curves (flat instead of Keplerian), gravitational lensing maps, CMB power spectrum,
large-scale structure. No Standard Model particle fits.

---

## Standard Explanation

Unknown particle beyond the Standard Model. Candidates include WIMPs (weakly interacting
massive particles), axions, sterile neutrinos. None detected despite extensive searches.
ΛCDM (Lambda + Cold Dark Matter) fits observations extremely well as a phenomenological model.
